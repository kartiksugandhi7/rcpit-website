Welcome to RCPIT

R. C. Patel Institute of Technology (RCPIT) is a reputed autonomous engineering institute located in Shirpur, Maharashtra. The institute focuses on academic excellence, innovation, research, and industry-oriented education.

About the Institute

Established in 2001 by Shirpur Education Society, RCPIT is approved by AICTE and affiliated with Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University (DBATU), Lonere.

The institute provides quality technical education with modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, and strong industry collaboration.

Vision

To achieve technical excellence by providing quality education, innovation, research, and ethical values.

Mission

  • Provide outcome-based education
  • Promote innovation and research
  • Develop skilled, ethical engineers
  • Contribute to nation building

Eligibility criteria and admission schedules are announced every academic year.

The Training & Placement Cell supports students through career guidance, skill development programs, internships, and campus placements.
